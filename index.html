02The Core Operational Challenge

Managing critical operations via disjointed spreadsheets and individual memory creates substantial business risk.

While functional initially, this approach introduces severe vulnerabilities, such as data misdirection or operational bottlenecks when key personnel are unavailable. These challenges represent significant business risks that require proactive mitigation.

Baseline resolves this in three fixed-price stages. An Ops Audit maps your dispatch, invoicing, and scheduling workflows and identifies where they break down. A Baseline Build implements the fixes as software engineered around how you already operate, rather than forcing you into a rigid enterprise platform. Baseline Care then keeps the system hosted, monitored, and maintained. Every build undergoes security review for data exposure and access vulnerabilities prior to deployment.

07FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who retains intellectual property rights?

Full source code and ownership transfer directly to your organization upon final invoice settlement, with zero licensing fees or platform dependencies.

Do I have to commit to a build before the audit?

No. The Ops Audit is a standalone, fixed-price engagement. The report is written so any competent developer could implement its recommendations — though most clients continue directly into a Baseline Build using the fixed quote included with the report.

What post-launch support options are available?

Complimentary post-deployment remediation is included for 30 days. Ongoing hosting, security monitoring, and small changes are handled through the Baseline Care retainer.

Are services delivered on-site or remotely?

Development is conducted via a remote-first methodology. On-site coordination for project initiation or final delivery is available for businesses located within the greater Indianapolis region.

What do security services require before work begins?

A signed authorization agreement defining exactly which systems are in scope. Scanning and phishing simulations are only ever performed against systems and staff you own or manage and have authorized in writing — this agreement is part of onboarding for every security engagement.

What is the standard response time framework?

All client communications receive a response within one business day or less.
