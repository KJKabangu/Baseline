02The problem

Operations are currently restricted to spreadsheets and individual memory.

While this approach may function temporarily, it introduces significant risks, such as data being transmitted to the wrong recipient or operational halts when a key employee is absent. This is an operational vulnerability that requires immediate attention.

I build custom tools tailored to your team's specific workflow, avoiding the complexity of unnecessary enterprise platforms. Because these systems house sensitive client records and operational data, a rigorous security audit for data exposure and access vulnerabilities is performed prior to deployment.
