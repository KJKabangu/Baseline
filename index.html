02The problem

You're running jobs out of spreadsheets and one person's memory.

It works — until the spreadsheet gets emailed to the wrong person, or the one employee who "just knows" the system is out for the week. That's not a tooling quirk you can put off forever. It's a business risk with your name on it.

I build the tool that replaces the spreadsheet — sized to how your team actually works, not an enterprise platform you'll grow into someday. And because it's going to hold your customers' names, job details, and records, I audit it for exposed data and access holes before it ever touches them, not after something goes wrong.
